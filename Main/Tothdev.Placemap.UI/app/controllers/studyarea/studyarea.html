Assessing the places and streets in my community. What do I like, what don't I like and what can be done?
Placemapping is a process that allows individuals to provide input on the quality of public and private spaces, streets, transit stops and any other element that helps shape your neighborhood or community. In every community, there are high performing cherished places that contribute to your quality of life, such as parks, Main Streets or waterfronts. There are also places that we wish could be made better; often these are streets which can be too wide and are barriers to movement and/or appreciation of an area.
This Placemapping site will allow you the opportunity to express your feelings directly into an application that will gather and synthesize the input, which can then be used to advise our leaders on quality places that need to be leveraged as well as lower performing places or streets that should be improved.
As you work through this application you would be allocated a total of 15 markers, which you can distribute as your choose between favorite places, not so favorite and opportunity places.
